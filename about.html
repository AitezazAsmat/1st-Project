WHO WE ARE

Company Profile

Lucide LED Lights was established to revolutionize the lighting industry with innovative LED technology. With over 19 years of experience, Lucide has developed intelligent lighting control products and systems. Our focus is always on quality, efficiency, and bringing charm to energy-saving lighting solutions. We have achieved over 90 patents and received numerous awards for innovation in the lighting industry.

Culture

Mission Statement:
Command each lamp and all the lights to save energy while delivering artistic lighting solutions.

Our Philosophy:
We believe in keeping innovative thinking at the forefront and providing outstanding products and services to meet the growing demand for energy-efficient lighting solutions worldwide.
